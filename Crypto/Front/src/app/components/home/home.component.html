Welcome to Crypto !

This is a very simple social network that respects totally your privacy :
  • All data (except your name) are encrypted with AES algorithm before they are sent over the network, and decrypted before they are displayed, using the key that you provide in the top left corner.
  • No personal information is never asked : no email address, no phone number... You just sign up with a login and a password, and an optional location. Your session expires after the duration that you specified.
When you use Crypto, you are the only responsible of your encryption key : tell it to trusted people only, and only the people that know it will ever be able to read your communications.
