Welcome to Hashgard !

This is a very simple social network that respects totally your privacy :
  • All data (except your name) are encrypted with AES algorithm before they are sent over the network, and decrypted before they are displayed, using the key that you provide in the top left corner. Https protocol adds another security layer.
  • No personal information is never asked : no email address, no phone number... You just sign up with a login and a password.
  • No censorship : the only one able to delete an article or a comment it the one who wrote it.
When you use Hashgard, you are the only responsible of your encryption key : tell it to trusted people only, and only the people that know it will ever be able to read your communications.
This is the very beginning of this project. Feel free to give me remarks :
  • Set the key "Hello !" (respect the caps).
  • Browse categories, "Projet Hashgard", then create the ones with subjects you want to talk about.
  • Write new posts in the topic you want, comment existing ones, and react to posts and comments.
Have fun !
